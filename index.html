About Macro Kold Inc.

Founded in 2005, Macro Kold Inc. has established itself as a leading provider of refrigeration and air conditioning solutions in Metro Manila and surrounding areas.

Our team of certified technicians and engineers brings decades of combined experience in designing, installing, and maintaining cooling systems for various industries.

We pride ourselves on delivering reliable, energy-efficient solutions that meet our clients' specific requirements while adhering to the highest industry standards.

At Macro Kold Inc., customer satisfaction is our top priority. We work closely with our clients to understand their needs and provide tailored solutions that deliver optimal performance and value.
